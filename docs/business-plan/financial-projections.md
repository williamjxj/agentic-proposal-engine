# Financial Projections - Agentic Proposal Engine
## Detailed 3-Year Financial Analysis for Government Loan Application

**Company**: Best IT Consulting  
**Business**: Agentic Proposal Engine (AI Proposal Automation SaaS)  
**Location**: Surrey, British Columbia, Canada  
**Prepared**: March 11, 2026  
**Financial Period**: Year 1 (2026), Year 2 (2027), Year 3 (2028)  

---

## Executive Summary

This document provides detailed financial projections for Agentic Proposal Engine, a SaaS platform that automates proposal writing for freelancers using AI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology.

**Key Financial Highlights**:
- **Requested Funding**: $150,000 CAD (loan)
- **Revenue Trajectory**: $75,600 (Y1) → $264,240 (Y2) → $624,000 (Y3)
- **Break-Even**: Month 18
- **3-Year Cumulative Revenue**: $963,840 CAD
- **3-Year Cumulative Profit**: $235,440 CAD (after loan repayments)
- **ROI for Business**: 157% by Year 3
- **Gross Margins**: 78% (Y1) → 85% (Y3)
- **Cash Position (End Y3)**: $275,440 CAD

**Funding Purpose**:
- **Product Development**: 40% ($60,000) - Backend engineering, AI optimization
- **Team Hiring**: 25% ($37,500) - Marketing specialist, customer success
- **Marketing**: 20% ($30,000) - Google Ads, content, partnerships
- **Operations**: 15% ($22,500) - Legal, accounting, infrastructure
