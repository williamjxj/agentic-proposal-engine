## 1. Federal Programs

### 1.1 BDC (Business Development Bank of Canada) - Small Business Loan

**Program Type**: Term Loan  
**Website**: [bdc.ca](https://www.bdc.ca)  
**Loan Amount**: $50,000 - $300,000  
**Interest Rate**: Prime + 2-4% (currently ~8-10%)  
**Term**: 3-10 years  
**Grace Period**: Negotiable (typically 6-12 months)

**Eligibility**:
- ✅ Canadian-owned business
- ✅ Revenue <$10M (Agentic: Pre-revenue, qualifies)
- ✅ Tech/innovation focus (SaaS qualifies strongly)
- ✅ Viable business plan (this document)
- ✅ Founder commitment (skin in the game)

**Use of Funds**:
- ✅ Technology development (AI/ML)
- ✅ Working capital
- ✅ Team salaries
- ✅ Marketing
- ❌ Debt repayment

**Application Process**:
1. Online application via BDC portal
2. Upload business plan, financials, founder resume
3. Initial review (2-3 weeks)
4. Meeting with BDC advisor (1-2 hours)
5. Due diligence (credit check, business validation)
6. Approval decision (4-8 weeks total)
7. Funding disbursed (1-2 weeks post-approval)

**Required Documents**:
- Business plan (✅ this document)
- 3-year financial projections (✅ created)
- Founder resume (✅ LinkedIn profile)
- Personal financial statement
- Articles of incorporation (if incorporated)
- Personal guarantee

**Collateral**:
- Intellectual property (codebase, algorithms)
- Personal guarantee from founder
- Potentially home equity (optional, depends on amount)

**Advantages**:
- ✅ Tech-focused: BDC specializes in tech startups
- ✅ Patient capital: Flexible repayment terms
- ✅ Advisory services: Free mentorship, workshops
- ✅ High approval rate: ~70% for tech companies with solid plans

**Disadvantages**:
- ⚠️ Interest 6-10% (moderate cost)
- ⚠️ Personal guarantee required
- ⚠️ Long application process (6-10 weeks)

**Strategic Fit**: **9/10** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐  
**Recommendation**: **PRIMARY APPLICATION**

### 1.2 NRC IRAP (Industrial Research Assistance Program)

**Program Type**: Non-Repayable Grant  
**Website**: [nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/eng/irap](https://nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/eng/irap)  
**Grant Amount**: $50,000 - $100,000+ (project-based)  
**Funding**: 80% of eligible R&D costs  
**Term**: 12-24 months per project

**Eligibility**:
- ✅ Canadian SME (≤500 employees, Agentic: 1 employee, qualifies)
- ✅ Technology innovation (AI/ML qualifies)
- ✅ R&D project (GraphRAG, autonomous features qualify)
- ✅ For-profit
- ✅ Intellectual property development

**Qualifying Projects for Agentic**:
1. **GraphRAG Implementation** - Advanced retrieval with knowledge graphs ($50k project)
2. **Multi-Agent Autonomous System** - LangGraph supervisor + CrewAI agents ($75k project)
3. **Cross-Platform API Integration** - Upwork, Freelancer, Fiverr unified API ($30k project)

**Eligible Costs**:
- ✅ Salaries (engineers, researchers)
- ✅ Contract R&D (universities, consultants)
- ✅ Materials (cloud compute, API costs)
- ❌ Marketing, sales, general operations

**Application Process**:
1. Contact local IRAP advisor (BC: Vancouver or Surrey office)
2. Initial meeting (1-2 hours, free consultation)
3. Submit project proposal (10-15 pages)
4. Technical review (6-8 weeks)
5. Advisor recommendation
6. Approval decision (3-6 months total)
7. Funding disbursed in milestones (quarterly)

**Required Documents**:
- Technical project proposal
- Budget breakdown
- Company financials
- Founder/team bios
- Proof of R&D capacity

**Reporting**:
- Quarterly progress reports
- Final technical report
- Financial statements

**Advantages**:
- ✅ **Non-repayable** (grant, not loan)
- ✅ Validates technology innovation
- ✅ Advisor support (technical guidance)
- ✅ Can combine with loans (BDC + IRAP common)

**Disadvantages**:
- ⚠️ Long application process (3-6 months)
- ⚠️ Requires clear R&D project (not general operations)
- ⚠️ Milestone-based funding (not lump sum)

**Strategic Fit**: **8/10** ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
**Recommendation**: **APPLY AFTER BDC LOAN** (complementary, not repayable)

## 2. Provincial Programs (British Columbia)

### 2.1 Innovate BC - Ignite Program

**Program Type**: Repayable Contribution (0% interest, revenue-based repayment)  
**Website**: [innovatebc.ca/programs/ignite](https://innovatebc.ca/programs/ignite)  
**Funding Amount**: $75,000 - $300,000  
**Repayment**: 5% of gross revenue until principal + 15% premium repaid

**Eligibility**:
- ✅ BC-based (Surrey qualifies)
- ✅ Tech company (SaaS qualifies)
- ✅ Pre-revenue or early-revenue (<$2M)
- ✅ Growth potential
- ✅ Innovative product/service

**Use of Funds**:
- ✅ Product development
- ✅ Team salaries
- ✅ Marketing (go-to-market)
- ✅ Operations

**Application Process**:
1. Online eligibility quiz
2. Submit application (25-page template)
3. Pitch presentation (20 min + Q&A)
4. Due diligence (financial, technical review)
5. Decision (4-6 months)
6. Funding disbursed in tranches (3-4 milestone payments)

**Required Documents**:
- Business plan
- Financial model
- Pitch deck
- Founder bios
- Product demo (if available)

**Repayment Example** (Agentic):
- **Funding**: $150,000
- **Repayment obligation**: $150,000 + 15% = $172,500
- **Year 1 revenue**: $75,600 × 5% = $3,780 paid
- **Year 2 revenue**: $264,240 × 5% = $13,212 paid
- **Year 3 revenue**: $624,000 × 5% = $31,200 paid
- **Total after 3 years**: $48,192 paid, **$124,308 remaining** (deferred)

**Advantages**:
- ✅ 0% interest (better than BDC)
- ✅ Repayment only when revenue positive
- ✅ BC-focused (understands regional market)

**Disadvantages**:
- ⚠️ Repayment obligation (not grant)
- ⚠️ Competitive (acceptance rate ~20%)
- ⚠️ Long application process

**Strategic Fit**: **7/10** ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
**Recommendation**: **ALTERNATIVE TO BDC** (if prefer revenue-based repayment)

## 4. Tax Credits & Incentives

### 4.1 SR&ED (Scientific Research & Experimental Development)

**Program Type**: Tax Credit  
**Website**: [canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/scientific-research-experimental-development-tax-incentive-program](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/scientific-research-experimental-development-tax-incentive-program.html)  
**Credit Amount**: 15-35% of eligible R&D expenditures  
**Refundable**: Yes (for CCPCs with <$500k annual taxable income)

**Eligibility**:
- ✅ Canadian-controlled private corporation (CCPC)
- ✅ R&D conducted in Canada (AI development qualifies)
- ✅ Systematic investigation (not routine)

**Qualifying Activities for Agentic**:
- ✅ RAG algorithm development
- ✅ GraphRAG implementation
- ✅ Multi-agent orchestration (LangGraph)
- ✅ NLP fine-tuning for proposals
- ❌ Front-end UI design (not R&D)
- ❌ Marketing, sales

**Eligible Expenditures**:
- Salaries (engineers, data scientists)
- Contract R&D
- Materials (cloud compute, APIs)

**Credit Calculation Example** (Year 1):
- **R&D Salaries**: $36,000 (backend engineer)
- **Cloud/API costs**: $7,200
- **Total eligible**: $43,200
- **Tax credit (35% for CCPC)**: $43,200 × 0.35 = **$15,120 refund**

**Application**:
- Filed with annual corporate tax return (T2 + form T661)
- Requires detailed project descriptions
- CRA review (3-12 months for first-time claims)

**Strategic Fit**: **8/10** ⭐⭐⭐⭐  
**Recommendation**: **APPLY Year 1-3** (recurring benefit, $15-50k/year refund)
